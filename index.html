About Us

Bhūmi was born from a love of hosting friends and making beautiful things by hand. After seeing how much joy people found in simple creative gatherings, I decided to turn that feeling into a business. Today, Bhūmi is your best friend for unforgettable get‑togethers. From cozy craft nights to curated gift hampers, we bring warmth, creativity and connection to every occasion. Our mission is to provide inviting spaces where friends and colleagues can relax, explore their artistic side, and share meaningful moments without the stress of planning.

Our Services

  • Creative Workshops
  • Events & Celebrations
  • Gifts & Hampers

Enjoy relaxed sessions in painting, jewelry making, candle crafting, and more. We provide all the materials and guidance—you bring the friends.

Hire us for weddings, birthdays or any special occasion to create a memorable creative corner. We set up interactive art stations so your guests can relax, connect and craft keepsakes to take home. It’s like having your bestie host the fun part of your event! You can also add our handmade items—like handwoven scarves or jewelry—to your event packages for an extra special touch.

Looking for the perfect thoughtful gift? Bhūmi curates beautiful hampers filled with artisanal treats, self‑care goodies, and custom keepsakes. Perfect for bridesmaids, birthdays, or just to show you care.
