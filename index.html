About Us

Bhūmi is your best friend for unforgettable gatherings. From cozy craft nights to curated gift hampers, we bring warmth, creativity and connection to every occasion. Our mission is to provide inviting spaces where friends and colleagues can relax, explore their artistic side, and share meaningful moments without the stress of planning.

Experiences

Creative Workshops

Enjoy relaxed sessions in painting, jewelry making, candle crafting, and more. We provide all the materials and guidance—you bring the friends.

Team Building

Boost collaboration and morale with custom group activities designed for corporate events. Choose from our menu of workshops or let us tailor one to your team.

Private Parties

Celebrate birthdays, bridal showers, or just because. We transform any space into a cozy studio and handle the setup, supplies, and cleanup.

Events & Celebrations

Hire us for weddings, birthdays or any special occasion to create a memorable creative corner. We set up interactive art stations so your guests can relax, connect and craft keepsakes to take home. It’s like having your bestie host the fun part of your event!

Gifts & Hampers

Looking for the perfect thoughtful gift? Bhūmi curates beautiful hampers filled with artisanal treats, self‑care goodies, and custom keepsakes. Perfect for bridesmaids, birthdays, or just to show you care.
